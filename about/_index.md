Starshard is Macheng Shen's personal agent stack — a substrate where memory is the primary, agents and tools are peripherals, and each user runs their own complete instance. This site is its public surface: long-form essays under `/essays/`, a continuous receipts log under `/receipts/`, and the Constraint Manifest at `/manifest/`.

Contact: [macshen93@gmail.com](mailto:macshen93@gmail.com). Code: [github.com/MachengShen](https://github.com/MachengShen). Threads: [x/@MachengShen](https://x.com/MachengShen).

Mail to the contact address lands inside the system described here — feedback gets classified, becomes a todo, or triggers a reply (possibly drafted by the agent, with my edits). That recursion is intentional.
